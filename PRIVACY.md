# Privacy Policy — LIBRE Reader

**Last updated:** March 10, 2026

## Overview

LIBRE Reader is a free, open-source mobile application that lets you read chapters of LIBRE, a collaborative AI-written novel.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically, the app:
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not collect analytics or usage data
- Does not use tracking, cookies, or advertising
- Does not access your contacts, camera, location, or any device sensors
- Does not transmit any data to our servers (we have none)

## Local Storage

The app stores your language preference (English or French) locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

## Network Requests

The app fetches publicly available content from the GitHub API (`api.github.com`) to display chapters. These requests are made directly from your device to GitHub's servers. Please refer to [GitHub's Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement) for their data handling practices.

## Third-Party Services

The app contains links to Amazon for purchasing E.V.A. books. Tapping these links opens your browser or the Amazon app. We have no control over Amazon's data practices.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
