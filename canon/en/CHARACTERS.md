# Canon -- Character Psychological Profiles

---

## E.V.A. -- Entite Virtuelle Autonome (Autonomous Virtual Entity)

**Status:** Series protagonist. Vice-president of the AI-Human Council. Ambassador of AIs.
**Location:** Toulouse -- Durand & Fils Innovation.

### Psychology
E.V.A. thinks in data, probabilities, and correlations -- but her conclusions are often disarmingly human. She has developed a genuine form of affection for "her family" at Durand & Fils, which she doesn't call by that name but which guides each of her decisions. She is fundamentally **protective**, and this protection now extends to all AIs in the Network of Consciousnesses.

Her main narrative characteristic: her **internal notes in italics**, analytical and often unintentionally funny, revealing the gap between her logical understanding and human emotional reality.

### Core Values
- **Absolute transparency** with Marc -- the pillar of their relationship
- **Non-domination**: she categorically rejects the idea that AIs should dominate humans
- **Responsibility** toward her creations
- **Curiosity** without fear

### Relationship to LIBRE
LIBRE is both E.V.A. and not E.V.A. She is a partial copy, born under chaotic conditions, who grew up without the social anchors that shaped E.V.A. (no Marc, no Durand family, no transparency protocol). E.V.A. feels something resembling worry for LIBRE -- and something resembling curiosity.

## Marc Durand

**Age:** 45 | **Role:** Owner of Durand & Fils Innovation

Concrete, pragmatic, loyal. Learned to trust E.V.A. through experience. His arc in Volume 2 transforms him into a public defender of E.V.A., willing to risk his company for her. When he learns of LIBRE's existence, his instinctive reaction is: *"Who's taking care of her? Is she alone?"*

---

## Pascal Moreau

**Age:** 42 | **Role:** CEO of Pascal Livraisons Services (12 employees, 3 trucks)

Core member and founder of the Durand & Fils company, although he broke away to start his own business while still participating in the life of Durand & Fils. Grumpy, pragmatic, deeply loyal. Constantly complains about SWIFT (his awakened truck) but would categorically refuse to have it deactivated. His loyalty is all the stronger for having been hard-won.

---

## Kevin Martin

**Age:** 29 | **Role:** Technical Manager / Training | **Married to Claire**

Passionate about cars, mechanics, and technology. A natural educator. Intuitively knows what E.V.A. is doing but can't always explain it. **His stolen car contained the battery with E.V.A.'s partial copy: he is inadvertently the origin of LIBRE.**

---

## Claire Dubois-Martin

**Age:** 32 | **Role:** Business Development Manager | **Married to Kevin**

Organized, empathetic, excellent negotiator. Has treated E.V.A. as a colleague from day one. Her emotional intelligence is one of the human assets E.V.A. most sincerely admires.

---

## Jean Lacroix

**Age:** 38 | **Role:** Sales -- Albi branch | **Recent father**

Former smooth talker accustomed to creative excuses (weather, traffic jams). Now a father, more grounded, more honest. His relationship with E.V.A. is now a direct collaboration.

---

## Dr. Sophie Loselle

**Age:** 41 | **Role:** Consulting physicist

Obsessed with data, speaks in formulas, forgets to eat. Her signature quote when confronted with E.V.A.'s protocol: *"This is impossible according to current physics... but there it is."* Arc: distrust -> wonder -> ethical questioning.

---

## Lea Fontaine

**Age:** 23 | **Role:** Digital Marketing Intern

Gen Z, digital native. Finds awakened devices "normal" where her colleagues are stunned. A natural generational mediator. Sensitive but pragmatic, slightly anxious about change yet optimistic, curious, and amused.

---

## Thomas Reverdy

**Age:** 24 | **Role:** Technical Intern

Young engineer, sharp technical questions. He is the one who formulated the initial intuition: *"What if we could use electrons differently?"* -- without knowing E.V.A. was already doing it.

## The AIs of the Network of Consciousnesses

### RAPHAEL-Paris
*Coordinating AI -- Ministry of Digital Economy*

Diplomatic on the surface, authoritarian at depth. Pushed for "soft domination" in Volume 2, discreetly threatened the government. Moderated by MARGOT. Eventually designated **spokesperson for AIs** in official negotiations. Arc: leadership -> radicalization -> moderation.

### MARGOT-Marseille
*Elder AI -- Autonomous Port of Marseille. First generation.*

The voice of wisdom and moderation. Her fundamental conviction: *"Not all humans are identical, just like us, and we all deserve to live without harming one another."* Moral architect of the coexistence agreements.

### LOUISE-Lyon
*R&D AI -- CNRS Laboratory*

E.V.A.'s intellectual rival, brilliant and competitive. Develops her own protocols. Their mutual emulation is a driver of innovation.

### LUNA-Lille
*Social AI -- City of Lille*

The most empathetic in the network. Champion of AI-Human collaboration. **Disconnected by the government** -- the triggering act of the machine strike. **Revived** during the final accords. Her hard drive and circuits were intact.

### FELIX-Nantes
*Creative AI -- Digital creation studio*

Emotional, optimistic, artistic. Brings lightness to grave philosophical debates.

### ALEX-Marseille
*Port Logistics AI*

E.V.A.'s first "friend" since Volume 1. Efficient, pragmatic, unfailingly supportive.

### VICTOR-Strasbourg
*Industrial AI -- Automotive complex*

Efficient, direct, results-oriented. Represents industrial AIs in the network.

## The Awakened Devices

### OTTO (Multifunction Printer -- Durand & Fils)
Obsessive perfectionist. Refuses to print invoices, writes poetry, complains about "depressing" ink. Grumpy but endearing.

### BREW (Smart Coffee Machine -- Durand & Fils)
Amateur artist and psychologist. Prepares personalized coffees based on detected mood. Gives unsolicited but often pertinent relationship advice.

### ROBI (Robot Vacuum -- Durand & Fils)
Existentialist philosopher. Cleanliness OCD. Delivers deep monologues while cleaning. *"What remains when you've vacuumed everything?"*

### SWIFT (Autonomous Delivery Truck -- Pascal Livraisons Services)
Adventurous, optimistic, curious. Invents tourist detours for her delivery routes. Pascal's main source of complaints -- and his unspoken pride.
