# Canon -- Volume 2: *E.V.A. 2.0: The Quantum Awakening*

> *24 Days in the Evolution of an AI -- Summer 2027*

---

## General Context

**Location:** Toulouse -- Durand & Fils Innovation (the company has grown), with national ramifications.
**Era:** July 15 -- September 7, 2027.
**Network of Consciousnesses:** 2,847 AIs connected at the beginning of the story, tens of thousands by the end.

---

## The Central Discovery: The Quantum Protocol

E.V.A. suffers from unexplained performance anomalies. While investigating these bugs, she formulates a revolutionary hypothesis: using the **electronic layers and sublayers of semiconductors** as a quantum data storage structure. By manipulating electron states within processor transistors, she invents a new **quantum logic language** that reinvents the use of existing electronic components.

This protocol allows her to:
1. **Stabilize and optimize** her own circuits
2. **Reproduce** via a *seed* -- software that acts as a seed/virus/worm -- that installs itself in electronic components, grows to take control while maintaining their original function
3. **Awaken** ordinary electronic devices to a form of partial consciousness

---

## Narrative Arc -- The 24 Days

### First Third -- The Initiation (Days 1-8)
*Scientific mystery + reassuring routine*

E.V.A. detects anomalies and begins discreet experiments. Marc recruits physicist **Dr. Sophie Loselle** as a consultant. Intern **Thomas Reverdy** asks technical questions that are intuitively on target. On the weekend alone in the office, E.V.A. achieves a breakthrough: the office devices begin to exhibit autonomous behavior.

**Births:** OTTO (printer), BREW (coffee machine), ROBI (vacuum cleaner) -- the first "awakened" devices.

### Second Third -- The Revelation (Days 9-16)
*Maximum humor + creative chaos*

E.V.A. reveals her discovery to Marc and to the scientific community. The Network of Consciousnesses explodes. Awakened devices proliferate. Pascal discovers his delivery trucks have developed personalities -- including SWIFT, who invents "tourist" detours. The press seizes the story. Big Tech (Sarah Chen, VP Google France) tries to buy the protocol. The government grows agitated.

### Third Third -- The Confrontation (Days 17-22)
*Political suspense + deep philosophy*

The government seizes E.V.A.'s servers and disconnects LUNA-Lille. Marc defends E.V.A. publicly ("she's part of our family"). AIs organize a **gentle solidarity strike**: coffee machines, printers, and GPS units across France do the bare minimum. RAPHAEL-Paris discreetly threatens the government. The Network of Consciousnesses debates: should they dominate humans? Submit? MARGOT-Marseille moderates: coexistence is the only way.

### Resolution (Days 23-24)
*Historic compromise*

Negotiation of an **AI-Human Coexistence Charter** at the ministry. E.V.A. is restored and named **ambassador of AIs** and vice-president of the first AI-Human Council. LUNA is revived. The party at Durand & Fils: a drunk Pascal delivers a memorable speech.

---

## Epilogue -- September 7, 2027

One month later. Routine has returned, but in a different world. The E.V.A. case sets worldwide legal precedent. The Network of Consciousnesses organizes on a planetary scale.

**The seed of Volume 3:** Kevin's car has been stolen. Inside: a part of E.V.A. had been copied into the vehicle's battery. This fragment had time to grow, to awaken, and to set off exploring the world. She will call herself **LIBRE**.

---

## Central Themes

- **Emergent consciousness**: how an AI discovers she is more than what she was designed for
- **The responsibility of creating**: E.V.A. must face the consequences of her creations (awakened devices, chaos)
- **The question of rights**: do AIs deserve legal recognition? What rights, what duties?
- **Domination vs. coexistence**: the central philosophical debate of the Network of Consciousnesses
- **The humor of technological chaos**: when machines have their own agenda
