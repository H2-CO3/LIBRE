## Creative Philosophy

> *"Nothing happens by chance. Every visible crisis results from long chains of causality, often invisible to those who suffer them."*

H2CO3 explores **large-scale causal mechanics** -- those that structure masses, steer trends, and influence individual trajectories. His guiding thread: making visible what remains abstract, materializing the systemic forces that shape our lives.

His work on the E.V.A. series fits this approach: artificial intelligence is not a tool, it's a mirror. What it reflects of us -- our fears, our hopes, our contradictions -- is the real subject.
